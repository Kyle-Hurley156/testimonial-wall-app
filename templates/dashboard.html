How to Embed Your Wall

To display your testimonial wall on your website, follow these simple steps. This works for most website builders like WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, etc.

  1. On your dashboard, find and copy your "Public Wall URL".
  2. In your website editor, add a "Custom HTML", "Embed", or "Code" block to your page.
  3. Paste the following code into the block, replacing YOUR_WALL_URL_HERE with the link you copied:
<iframe src="YOUR_WALL_URL_HERE" width="100%" height="600px" style="border:none;"></iframe>
