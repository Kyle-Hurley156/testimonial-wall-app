How to Embed Your Testimonial Wall

Displaying your wall on your website is like embedding a YouTube video. You just need to copy one line of code and paste it into your website's editor.

  1. Copy Your Embed Code: Below is the single line of code you need.
    <iframe src="{{ url_for('show_wall', user_id=current_user.id, _external=True) }}" width="100%" height="600px" style="border:none;"></iframe>
  2. Go to Your Website Editor: Log in to your website's admin panel (e.g., WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Shopify, etc.).
  3. Paste the Code: Find the page where you want the testimonials to appear. Add a "Custom HTML," "Embed," or "Code" block, and simply paste the code you copied. Save your changes.

That's it! Your beautiful wall of testimonials will now be live on your site.
